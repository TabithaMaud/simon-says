Learn how to play!

Simon Says Pick A Color is a game for players to test their memory skills.

The game starts with one of four colors lighting up, this will be the color that you will need to remember to click next. The second round the first color will light up again followed by a new color. You will then need to remember the order of the two colors and then click them in the correct sequence. Each new level one new color will be added to the end of the sequence.

Each time you make a correct selection your score will increase by one.
